Core Features

Blue Wave Player is built from the ground up to provide a seamless and accessible listening experience.

Designed for Accessibility

Full compatibility with screen readers, including NVDA and JAWS. Navigate the entire application using only your keyboard with intuitive shortcuts and clear, concise labels.

Audiobook & Podcast Support

Easily manage your library of audiobooks and subscribe to your favorite podcasts. The player remembers your position in every file, so you can always pick up where you left off.

Crystal Clear Audio

Our audio engine is optimized for voice clarity, ensuring you never miss a word. Supports a wide range of audio formats including MP3, M4A, WAV, and FLAC.

Simple Library Management

Organize your audio files into collections, view your listening history, and easily import new files. The interface is clean and designed to be straightforward.
