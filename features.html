Core Features

Blue Wave Player is built from the ground up to provide a seamless and accessible listening experience.

Designed for Accessibility

Full compatibility with screen readers like NVDA. Navigate the entire application using only your keyboard with intuitive shortcuts and clear, concise labels. With a screen reader, you can:

  • View the elapsed and remaining time of the current track.
  • View the elapsed and remaining time of the entire book.
  • See the total time for both the current track and the full book.
  • Easily jump to a specific track within the book.

Audiobook & Podcast Support

Easily manage your library of audiobooks and subscribe to your favorite podcasts. The player remembers your position in every file, so you can always pick up where you left off.

Crystal Clear Audio

Our audio engine is optimized for voice clarity, ensuring you never miss a word. Supports a wide range of audio formats including MP3, M4A, WAV, and FLAC.

Simple Library Management

Organize your audio files into collections, view your listening history, and easily import new files. The interface is clean and designed to be straightforward.

Sleep Timer

Set a timer to automatically stop playback after a certain period, perfect for listening before you sleep.

Rewind After Pause

Automatically rewinds a few seconds after you resume playback, helping you recall the context of what you were listening to.

Content Organization

Categorize your audiobooks and podcasts into custom collections to keep your library tidy and easy to navigate.

Variable Speed Playback

Adjust the playback speed to listen at your own pace, from a slower narration to a quick catch-up.

Bookmarks and Notes

Place bookmarks at important points in your audio and add notes to them for future reference.

Multi-Volume Support

Seamlessly manage and play audiobooks that are split into multiple files or volumes.

Podcast Offline Listening

Download your favorite podcast episodes directly within the app and listen to them anytime, even without an internet connection.
